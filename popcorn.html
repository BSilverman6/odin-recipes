Popcorn

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Popcorn
  • 1+ Tb Coconut Oil
  • 2 Tb Butter
  • Salty Spice Mix

Steps

  1. In a medium lidded pot over medium high heat, heat Coconut oil until melted
  2. Add 3 Kernels of Popcorn and gently shake the pot until Popcorn is coated in Oil
  3. When all Kernels are popped, add the remaining popcorn
  4. Gently Shake the Pot every few moments and continuously agitate when popcorn begins to pop
  5. Turn off the Heat when Popcorn is finished popping
  6. Drizzle with Melted Butter and a Salty Spice Mix then mix

Voila! Enjoy
